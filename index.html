About Me

I've been programming since I was 13 years old. Throughout the following years since, I've worked with Python, HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, C++, and YAML.

I believe that being self-taught in any field exposes you to how you really learn and prepares you for how you're going to have to learn for the rest of your life.

Earlier this year, in 2019, I decided I wanted to get a proper hands-on education after learning on my own and bring myself up to a level where I can be competitive against anyone else in the industry.

Upon completing this program, I am certain I will be able to stand out in the industry, be equipped to solve any problem, and master my ability to learn at a rapid pace.
